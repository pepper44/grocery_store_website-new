Our Vegetables are from the most organic farms over the country

Dambulla

One-fifth of our vegetables are from one of the major vegetable supplier in the country, Dambulla markets.

Picked from most organic farms in the fine city of Dambulla, which has the best temparature to grow fresh vegetables.

Welimada

Most of the carrots, cabbages and leeks you find on our racks are what we regularly buy from the finest farms at Welimada.

Growing amidst the perfect humid climate guarantees the vegetables have the best taste.

Jaffna

Farms in Jaffna, which has a decade old reputation for the finest potatoes and onions in the country,is our largest supplier for our potatoes and onions.

Assuaring the qualityy and freshness, our transpoort services bring them regularly from Jaffna to our supermarkets all over the country.

Individual Farmers

Being a helping hand, some of our vegetables that are uncommonly found in commercial farms, such as Tibbotu,Drumsticks and Yams are from individual farmers all over the county

They are registered suppliers in our company, and once the goods meet quality assuaring standards, our agents buy them. We ensure the farmers are happy, food doesn't go to waste and you get what you want.
